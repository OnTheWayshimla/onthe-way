Kasuli Holiday Package

Discover the charm of Kasuli with our exclusive holiday package. Enjoy the scenic beauty, peaceful atmosphere, and unique colonial architecture.

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Kasuli and transfer to hotel. Explore the local market in the evening.
  • Day 2: Visit Christ Church, Sunset Point, and Kasauli Brewery.
  • Day 3: Excursion to Monkey Point and Gilbert Trail.
  • Day 4: Free day for shopping and leisure. Departure in the evening.

Pricing

Starting from ₹12,000 per person. The price includes accommodation, breakfast, and guided tours. Additional costs may apply for activities and meals not mentioned in the itinerary.

Inclusions

  • Accommodation in a 3-star hotel
  • Daily breakfast
  • Guided sightseeing tours
  • All transfers by private vehicle
  • Assistance on arrival and departure

Exclusions

  • Travel insurance
  • Lunch and dinner
  • Entry fees to monuments and activities
  • Personal expenses and tips
  • Any item not mentioned in the inclusions
