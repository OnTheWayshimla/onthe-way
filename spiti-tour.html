Spiti Valley Holiday Package

Experience the rugged beauty of Spiti Valley with our exclusive package. Enjoy breathtaking landscapes, ancient monasteries, and unique cultural experiences.

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Manali and transfer to Spiti Valley. Overnight stay in Kaza.
  • Day 2: Visit Key Monastery and Kibber Village.
  • Day 3: Explore Tabo Monastery and Dhankar Fort.
  • Day 4: Return to Manali. Departure in the evening.

Pricing

Starting from ₹25,000 per person. The price includes accommodation, breakfast, and guided tours. Additional costs may apply for activities and meals not mentioned in the itinerary.

Inclusions

  • Accommodation in a 3-star hotel
  • Daily breakfast
  • Guided sightseeing tours
  • All transfers by private vehicle
  • Assistance on arrival and departure

Exclusions

  • Travel insurance
  • Lunch and dinner
  • Entry fees to monuments and activities
  • Personal expenses and tips
  • Any item not mentioned in the inclusions
