Dharamshala Holiday Package

Immerse yourself in the tranquility of Dharamshala with our exclusive holiday package. Enjoy the scenic landscapes, vibrant Tibetan culture, and serene atmosphere.

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Dharamshala and transfer to hotel. Explore McLeod Ganj in the evening.
  • Day 2: Visit Tsuglagkhang Complex, Dalai Lama Temple, and Bhagsu Waterfall.
  • Day 3: Excursion to Triund for panoramic views and trekking.
  • Day 4: Visit Dharamkot and return to hotel. Departure in the evening.

Pricing

Starting from ₹14,000 per person. The price includes accommodation, breakfast, and guided tours. Additional costs may apply for activities and meals not mentioned in the itinerary.

Inclusions

  • Accommodation in a 3-star hotel
  • Daily breakfast
  • Guided sightseeing tours
  • All transfers by private vehicle
  • Assistance on arrival and departure

Exclusions

  • Travel insurance
  • Lunch and dinner
  • Entry fees to monuments and activities
  • Personal expenses and tips
  • Any item not mentioned in the inclusions
