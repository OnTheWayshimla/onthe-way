Manali Holiday Package

Explore the enchanting beauty of Manali with our exclusive holiday package. Enjoy stunning landscapes, thrilling adventures, and serene environments.

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Manali and transfer to hotel. Explore Mall Road in the evening.
  • Day 2: Visit Solang Valley and Rohtang Pass for adventure activities.
  • Day 3: Sightseeing tour including Hidimba Temple, Manu Temple, and Vashisht Baths.
  • Day 4: Visit Old Manali and return to hotel. Departure in the evening.

Pricing

Starting from ₹18,000 per person. The price includes accommodation, breakfast, and guided tours. Additional costs may apply for activities and meals not mentioned in the itinerary.

Inclusions

  • Accommodation in a 3-star hotel
  • Daily breakfast
  • Guided sightseeing tours
  • All transfers by private vehicle
  • Assistance on arrival and departure

Exclusions

  • Travel insurance
  • Lunch and dinner
  • Entry fees to monuments and activities
  • Personal expenses and tips
  • Any item not mentioned in the inclusions
