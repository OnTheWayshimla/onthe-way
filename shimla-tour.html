Shimla Holiday Package

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Shimla, the queen of hills, with our exclusive Shimla Holiday Package. This package offers a perfect blend of nature, culture, and adventure, making it ideal for families, couples, and solo travelers.

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Shimla and transfer to hotel. Explore Mall Road in the evening.
  • Day 2: Full-day sightseeing tour including The Ridge, Jakhoo Temple, and Christ Church.
  • Day 3: Excursion to Kufri for adventure activities and panoramic views.
  • Day 4: Visit to the Summer Hill and return to hotel. Departure in the evening.

Pricing

Starting from ₹15,000 per person. The price includes accommodation, breakfast, and guided tours. Additional costs may apply for activities and meals not mentioned in the itinerary.

Inclusions

  • Accommodation in a 3-star hotel
  • Daily breakfast
  • Guided sightseeing tours
  • All transfers by private vehicle
  • Assistance on arrival and departure

Exclusions

  • Travel insurance
  • Lunch and dinner
  • Entry fees to monuments and activities
  • Personal expenses and tips
  • Any item not mentioned in the inclusions
